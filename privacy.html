English

Paintlet respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Paintlet does not directly collect personal data such as name, email address, or contact details.

However, we may collect non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Gameplay interactions and usage data
  • Ad interactions and reward activity
  • Purchase and restore status for in-app purchases

This information is used to improve the app experience, maintain game progress, provide rewards, and support optional purchases.

Advertising

Paintlet uses third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob, to display rewarded ads and interstitial ads.

AdMob may collect certain data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad impressions and interactions
  • Approximate location and diagnostic information where applicable

For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Paintlet offers optional in-app purchases such as coin packs, limitless features, boosters, and premium worlds. Purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store and Google Play. We do not store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

Paintlet does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
